Welcome to Finetunes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

We also collect information about your usage of the app, including the prompts you engage with and the songs you submit.

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including to personalize your experience, create weekly summaries, facilitate voting and community features, and communicate with you about updates and new features. We do not sell your personal information. Your song submissions and username may be visible to other Finetunes users as part of the community voting and discovery features. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information publicly or with our partners.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. You may also request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account entirely. For any privacy-related requests, please contact us at privacy@finetunes.app.

About Finetunes

Finetunes was born from a simple observation: music has an incredible power to connect us to moments in time. A single song can transport you back to your first day of college, a summer road trip with friends, or that quiet moment standing in the snow under a streetlamp.

Our Mission

We believe that music is more than just entertainment—it's a shared language of human experience. Finetunes creates a space where people can discover new music through the lens of relatable moments, those specific scenes and scenarios that shape who we are. Every week, we invite our community to submit songs inspired by prompts that capture these universal-yet-personal experiences.

How It Started

The idea for Finetunes emerged from late-night conversations about how certain songs become inseparable from specific memories. We realized that while music streaming services are great at algorithmic recommendations, they miss the human element—the stories, emotions, and shared experiences that make music truly meaningful. We wanted to build something that brings people together through these connections.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional music discovery apps, Finetunes isn't about genres or popularity charts. It's about moments. Our weekly prompts are carefully crafted to be specific enough to spark vivid memories, yet universal enough that everyone can relate. Whether you're remembering the anxiety of parallel parking or the peace of fresh snowfall, there's a song that captures that feeling—and probably hundreds of others who felt the same way.

Join Our Community

Finetunes is more than an app—it's a community of music lovers who understand that the best playlists aren't just collections of songs, but soundtracks to our lives. We're excited to have you here, sharing your memories and discovering the moments that others have set to music.
